## Key Findings

### 1. Both Sites Exceeded the Contractual Savings Threshold
The guaranteed minimum is 15%. YYZ3 averaged 17% and YYZ4 averaged 16%. The Smart Valve outperformed its commitment at every scale.

### 2. The Strongest Savings Came First
YYZ3's first full quarter of operation (Jul–Sep 2024) produced a **58.69% reduction** — the equivalent of eliminating more than half the site's peak summer water usage in a single quarter. This demonstrates that the system operates at peak efficiency immediately upon installation.

### 3. Performance Is Resilient to Operational Disruption
Large logistics facilities are inherently variable — headcount shifts, seasonal throughput peaks, enhanced sanitation protocols, and equipment changes all affect water usage independently of the Smart Valve. Despite two quarters of operational anomalies at each site, the portfolio average held at 16.5%, above the guarantee. This is a strong indicator of the technology's robustness.

### 4. Flow Rate Reduction Is Significant
At YYZ3, the Smart Valve reduced flow rate from **5.26 to 2.74 gal/min** — a 2.52 gal/min (47.9%) reduction. This level of flow rate optimization at a single facility has substantial downstream implications for water infrastructure stress, maintenance intervals, and peak demand fees.

### 5. The Anomaly at YYZ4 Was Human, Not Technical
The Q4 2024 anomaly at YYZ4 occurred because the valve was physically removed from the system in September 2024. Once reinstalled in October, subsequent quarters returned immediately to double-digit savings (17%, 23%, 13%). The technology itself never underperformed.

## Recommendations for Prospective Clients

1. **Start with your highest-usage facility.** The larger the water bill, the faster the payback. Large warehouses, hotels, and industrial facilities see the strongest ROI.

2. **Request a site audit.** Perfect Water Valve can assess your current flow rates and usage patterns to project facility-specific savings before any commitment.

3. **Consider a pilot.** Amazon started with two sites. The verified data from those sites is now the foundation for a broader deployment decision. A pilot gives you real numbers for your specific context.

4. **Factor in sewage costs.** Water savings on your bill are typically mirrored by sewage savings, which are often calculated based on consumption. Total utility savings are substantially higher than water savings alone.

5. **Ask about the guarantee.** The 15% minimum savings guarantee is contractual. If your facility doesn't hit 15%, you have recourse. The Amazon pilot exceeded this at every site where the valve was properly installed and maintained.
